💡 Quick Start Guide

This portal is designed for Team Leads and Operations Managers to manage task distribution, compliance timelines, field executive locations, and payouts in real-time.

📋 Actionable Workflow Tips

  • Assign Tasks: Go to the Task assigning tab, click "Assign" on any item in the Unassigned Queue, and select an active employee.
  • Employee Profile: Click any employee's row or details button to view their attendance history and performance ratings.
  • Claim Validations: In the Incentive tab, review pending payout commission claims. Clicking "Approve" adds the amount back to the Team Incentive Pool.
  • Excel / CSV Export: Use the "Export" buttons in any tab to generate spreadsheet files from database tables.

⌨ System Shortcuts

Shift + AOpen Task Assigning Dialog
Shift + SOpen Portal Settings
EscClose all open popups/drawers
